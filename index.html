Welcome Home

Asgard is more than just a guild; it is a community of passionate World of Warcraft players who come together to share their love for the game. Founded in 2015, Asgard has built a reputation for being a friendly and inclusive guild that welcomes players of all skill levels. We pride ourselves on our strong sense of camaraderie and teamwork, which has helped us achieve numerous accomplishments in both PvE and PvP content. Whether you're looking for a casual group to run dungeons with or a dedicated raiding team to tackle the toughest challenges, Asgard has something for everyone. Join us and become a part of our growing family!

Our "Skills"

Wiping in Raid

We proudly march into the newest raid content the moment it is released—bright-eyed, undergeared, and tragically overconfident. Our mission? To spend countless hours valiantly dying to the simplest mechanics known to Azeroth. Standing in fire? Absolutely. Missing interrupts? It is an art form. But through sheer stubbornness and the power of snack-fueled determination, we eventually down a boss… and then get to do it all over again! Join us in this glorious Asgardian tradition, where every raid night is a chance to wipe heroically, argue over loot diplomatically, and etch your name into the Halls of Valor—not for flawless execution, but for showing up, pressing buttons, and laughing through the chaos. Glory awaits. Probably. Maybe. No promises.

Bricking Keys

They say failure builds character, and if that is true, then after our Mythic+ runs, our guild is basically a Nobel Prize-winning, Oscar-holding, emotionally enlightened band of champions. We did not just fail—we redefined failure. Timers? Optional. Deaths? Frequent. Strategy? Mostly panic and yelling "lust!" at the wrong times. But by the end, we had bonded through shared catastrophe, stood proudly atop a pile of repair bills, and walked away with no loot, shattered egos, and a +2 key downgraded to something that insults us personally. And honestly? That is the real win. Mythic+ is not about victory—it is about shared trauma, questionable decisions, and bonding with our guildies.
